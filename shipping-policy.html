Shipping Policy

House Of Brands works primarily through retail, marketplace, wholesale and brand program channels. Shipping terms may vary by platform, partner or commercial arrangement.

Marketplace orders

Orders placed through marketplaces or retail partners are shipped according to that partner's shipping policy, delivery timelines and service coverage.

Business shipments

Bulk, OEM, wholesale or campaign shipments are handled as per the agreed purchase order, invoice, logistics partner and destination requirements.

Delays

Delivery timelines can be affected by stock availability, destination, customs, holidays, weather and logistics constraints.

Contact

For shipment questions, write to care@houseofbrands.in.
