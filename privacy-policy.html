Privacy Policy

House Of Brands respects the privacy of visitors, partners and customers. This page explains the standard information we may collect when you contact us or use our website.

Information we collect

We may collect your name, email address, phone number, company details and message content when you contact us for business, retail, sourcing or collaboration enquiries.

How we use it

We use submitted information to respond to enquiries, evaluate partnership requests, coordinate business communication and improve the relevance of our services.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared with internal teams or service partners only when needed to respond to your request or operate the website.
